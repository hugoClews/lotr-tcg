## ğŸ’¡ Strategy Tips

### As Fellowship Player
1. **Don't overcommit** - Every card you play gives Shadow twilight
2. **Time your moves** - Move to Sanctuaries when weak
3. **Protect Frodo** - He's your win condition
4. **Balance speed vs safety** - Double-moving is risky but wins games

### As Shadow Player
1. **Be patient** - Wait for big twilight pools
2. **Target weak companions** - Don't always go for Frodo
3. **Overwhelm when possible** - 2Ã— strength = instant kill
4. **Save NazgÃ»l for key moments** - They're expensive but deadly

### General
- **Card advantage matters** - Drawing cards is powerful
- **Know when to concede skirmishes** - Sometimes it's okay to lose one
- **Read the board state** - Count twilight, wounds, cards in hand
- **Bluff** - Your opponent can't see your hand

## ğŸ“š Quick Reference

### Turn Order
1. **Fellowship** - Play Free Peoples cards
2. **Shadow** - Opponent plays minions
3. **Maneuver** - Special abilities
4. **Archery** - Ranged combat

### Key Numbers
- **Twilight Cost** - Top right of card
- **Strength** - Combat power (sword icon)
- **Vitality** - Health (heart icon)
- **Resistance** - Corruption threshold (Ring-bearer only)
- **Shadow Number** - Twilight added when moving to site

### Keywords
- **Archer** - Deals archery damage
- **Fierce** - Can be assigned to Ring-bearer directly
- **Defender +X** - Can defend that many extra companions
- **Damage +X** - Deals extra wound when winning
- **Ranger** - Various terrain bonuses
- **Unique (â—†)** - Only one copy in play
